## Intent
Let participants tell the bot when they're busy, in all three shapes the product requires: a single busy date, a busy date range (multi-day), and a busy time range within a day or across a date range. Plus managing those entries (view / remove / clear). After this epic the bot has the raw availability data the engine needs.

## Features
- `FEAT-availability-input-1` — Busy dates & date ranges (whole-day)
- `FEAT-availability-input-2` — Busy time ranges (within days)
- `FEAT-availability-input-3` — Manage my busy times (view / remove / clear)
